What is "Wenig Licht"?

Low light means no direct sunlight and limited ambient light‚Äîlike a room with a north-facing window, or several feet from any window. If you can comfortably read without turning on a lamp, that's usually enough!

üèÜ Champions of Wenig Licht

ZZ Plant

The king of low light. Survives in offices with only fluorescent lighting. Glossy leaves stay beautiful with minimal care.
Snake Plant

Tolerates any light level from dark corners to bright sun. Grows slowly in low light but stays healthy.
Pothos

Thrives in low light, trailing beautifully. Variegation may fade in very dark spots, but it'll still grow strong.
Peace Lily

One of few flowering plants that bloom in low light. Elegant white flowers brighten dark corners.
Chinese Evergreen

Beautiful patterned leaves in various colors. One of the most tolerant of low light conditions.
Cast Iron Plant

Named for its toughness! Handles neglect, low light, and temperature changes like a champ.
