Best Houseplants for Beginners

Start your plant journey with these nearly indestructible varieties.

New to houseplants? These beginner-friendly varieties are forgiving of mistakes and thrive with minimal attention. Perfect for building your confidence before taking on more challenging plants.

The "Unkillable" Starter Plants

1. Pothos

The ultimate beginner plant. Tolerates low light, irregular watering, and tells you when it's thirsty by drooping slightly. Fast growing and rewarding.

2. Snake Plant

Survives weeks without water. Tolerates any light level. Practically thrives on neglect.

3. ZZ Plant

Water once a month (yes, really). Handles low light. Glossy leaves always look good.

4. Spider Plant

Hard to kill and produces babies you can share. Great for building confidence.

5. Rubber Plant

Bold statement plant that's surprisingly easy. Tolerates some neglect.

Beginner Tips

  • Underwater rather than overwater - Most plants die from overwatering
  • Check soil before watering - Stick your finger 1" deep; water only if dry
  • Start with low-light plants - They're generally more forgiving
  • Don't repot immediately - Let new plants adjust first
  • Learn one plant's needs before adding more
