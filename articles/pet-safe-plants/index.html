🐾 Pet Safety Updated Feb 2026

20 Pet-Safe Houseplants

Beautiful plants that won't harm your cats or dogs. Create a green home without worrying about your furry friends.

⚠️

Important Note

While these plants are non-toxic, eating large amounts of any plant can cause stomach upset. If your pet frequently chews plants, consider placing them out of reach.

🏆 Top Pet-Safe Plants

Spider Plant
Easy • Pet Safe ✓

Produces cute babies, purifies air. Cats love to play with it but it's completely safe!
Boston Fern
Medium • Pet Safe ✓

Lush, feathery fronds. Great for hanging baskets away from playful paws.
Calathea
Medium • Pet Safe ✓

Beautiful patterns, safe for all pets. Leaves move throughout the day!
Parlor Palm
Easy • Pet Safe ✓

Elegant palm, tolerates low light. Completely non-toxic to cats and dogs.
Peperomia
Easy • Pet Safe ✓

Many varieties with fun textures. Compact size perfect for shelves.
Areca Palm
Medium • Pet Safe ✓

Tropical vibes, great air purifier. Safe and beautiful statement plant.

🚫 Plants to Avoid with Pets

These popular plants are toxic to cats and dogs:

• Pothos / Devil's Ivy
• Monstera
• Snake Plant
• Peace Lily
• Philodendron
• Aloe Vera
• ZZ Plant
• Dieffenbachia

💡 Tips for Pet Owners

🪴 Use Hanging Planters

Keep plants out of reach with ceiling hooks or high shelves.

🍋 Citrus Deterrent

Cats dislike citrus. Place lemon peels around plants they're curious about.

🌿 Provide Cat Grass

Give cats their own plant to chew on and they'll leave yours alone.
