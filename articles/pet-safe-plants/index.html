Plants to Avoid with Pets

These common houseplants are toxic and should be kept away from pets:

  • Pothos - Causes mouth irritation and vomiting
  • Philodendron - Similar to pothos, toxic if ingested
  • Peace Lily - Can cause severe mouth burning
  • Snake Plant - Mildly toxic, causes nausea
  • Aloe Vera - Toxic to cats and dogs
  • Jade Plant - Can cause vomiting and depression
