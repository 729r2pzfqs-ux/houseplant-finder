Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best houseplants for beginners?

Pothos, Snake Plant, ZZ Plant, and Spider Plant are excellent for beginners. They tolerate neglect, adapt to various light conditions, and are very forgiving.

What houseplants are safe for cats and dogs?

Spider Plant, Boston Fern, Calathea, Parlor Palm, and Peperomia are all pet-safe. Browse all pet-safe plants ‚Üí

What are the best low light houseplants?

Snake Plant, ZZ Plant, Pothos, Peace Lily, and Chinese Evergreen thrive in low light conditions. Browse low light plants ‚Üí

How often should I water my houseplants?

It varies by plant. Most prefer to dry out slightly between waterings. Check the top inch of soil - if dry, water thoroughly. Succulents need water every 2-3 weeks, while tropical plants may need weekly watering.

What houseplants purify air the best?

Snake Plant, Pothos, Peace Lily, Spider Plant, and Chinese Evergreen are excellent air purifiers identified in NASA research.
